Experiment with Color Lab

During the class session, we delved into the topic of infographics and covered a wide range of topics including the definition and data visualzation, as well as the design process and guidelines for creating effective infographics. Furthermore, the class included several examples of infographics and a demonstration of the resources available for creating them, including various desktop applications and online tools. This section of figures showcases the importance of effective design in creating clear and visually appealing maps.

Figure 1: Autogenerate Map

Figure 1 contains layers of roads, trails, water bodies, and airports. The map includes interstates, a secondary highway layer that depicts main highways, and a local connecting road layer. The layer's symbology, such as color, was chosen to create visual contrast and complementarity between each other. The controlled-access highway layer was represented by thick, red lines, the secondary highway layer by thin brow lines. This color and line choice helps distinguish between the different layers on the map while also keeping the overall design visually harmonious. It is the result of experimenting with colors and automated symbology setting for lines, points, and polygons.

Figure 2: Contrast Map

Figure 2 shows a map with color harmony and contrast. We modified Figure 1 to use colors that are highly distinguishable from each other, striving to overdo the contrast. We were able to learn the importance of color contrast in creating effective maps, as well as gain hands-on experience with modifying code to achieve specific map styles. The map also shows the layers of different water bodies. The map includes lake/pond, reservoir. Each water body type was represented by different colors.

Figure 3: Harmony Map

Figure 3 is a result of modifying Figure 1 and 2 to create a harmonious map with selective contrast. The goal is to use colors that complement each other to achieve a visually pleasing result while still highlighting the most important features.
